# Realm 1 — The Crimson Hollow

> Living spec. Last updated: 2026-05-17. Built in code as "The Crimson Hollow" (cave platformer).

---

## What it is
A platformer realm. Cave-like. Curiosity moves through it left-to-right (or whatever the path turns out to be), jumping and navigating.

## The verb
Platforming — jumps, falls, ledges. It's an obby.

## What Curiosity collects
**Jade crystal pieces.** Scattered through the realm. Curiosity picks them up as they go.

## The puzzle
Getting to the end of the platformer = solving the realm. Collecting the jade pieces is part of doing that — the pieces aren't a side thing, they're the point.

## What happens at the end
1. Curiosity finishes the platformer.
2. Fade / transition back to the **hub**.
3. In the hub, **all the other doors are now locked**.
4. Curiosity **uses the jade pieces to forge a jade key**.
5. The jade key **unlocks Door 2**.

**This loop is Realm 1 only.** It's not the pattern for the whole game — Realm 2 onward will play and unlock differently. Realm 1 introduces the *idea* that what you carry out of a realm matters; later realms riff on that idea, they don't repeat it.
